## 🚨 Critical SEO Issues

### 1. Homepage Meta — Weak & Generic
- **Title:** "Lux Neon LED Fine Art Gallery" (35 chars — too short, no keywords)
- **Description:** "LED Fine Art Gallery located in Dania Beach, Fl" (48 chars — way too short, no value prop)
- **H1:** Missing or buried

**Recommended:**
- **Title:** "Lux Neon Art Gallery | LED Fine Art, Neon Signs & Event Venue | Dania Beach FL" (78 chars)
- **Description:** "South Florida's premier LED fine art gallery featuring neon art, custom signs, and pop art by local artists. Visit our Dania Beach gallery, rent our event space, or shop 350+ pieces online. Free shipping." (200 chars)

### 2. Cannibalization — "lux neon" Ranks on 7+ URLs
The brand keyword "lux neon" is ranking on 7 different URLs, causing Google to split authority:
- / (position 1)
- /collections (position 10)
- /collections/all (position — via "luxneon")
- /collections/all?page=2 (position 18)
- /blogs/news/why-lux-neon... (position 29)
- /collections/frontpage (position 46)
- /blogs/news/lux-neon-led... (position 57)

**Fix:** Canonicalize all to homepage. Add `rel=canonical` and internal linking strategy.

### 3. Duplicate/Similar Collection Names
Two collections both named "Neon Art":
- `/collections/neon-art` (smart collection, ID 518121193751)
- `/collections/neon-art-1` (smart collection, ID 520548679959)
- `/collections/neon-art-2` (custom collection, ID 520549499159)

**Fix:** Consolidate into ONE "Neon Art" collection. Redirect the others.

### 4. Products Missing Body/Description
Several products have 0 chars body_html:
- `bouts-bustop` (0 chars)
- `bouts-dolphins` (0 chars)
- `bouts-tyreek-hill` (0 chars)
- `mj-pop-frida-canvas-t-shirt` (0 chars)
- `mjpop-frida-coffee-mug` (0 chars)
- `genius-black-white` (0 chars)
- `genius-blue-flowers` (0 chars)
- `genius-yellow-daisy` (0 chars)

**Fix:** Every product needs 150+ word descriptions with keywords.

### 5. Products Missing product_type
Many products have blank `product_type`, which hurts:
- Google Shopping feed categorization
- Internal filtering/navigation
- Schema markup accuracy

Products with missing type include: 305-led-black-and-gold-end-table, always-hustlin-merch-pack, audrey-diamonds, bart-s-chaos-street-art-tee, and many more.

### 6. Multiple Draft Products Still Indexable
Draft products found: genius-black-hoodie, genius-black-white, genius-blue-flowers, genius-yellow-daisy, chucky-s-worst-nightmare, donalds-america-feature, several wine products (beckstoffer, coombsville, half-heaven).

**Review:** Either publish with proper descriptions or ensure they're not generating 404s from old links.

### 7. Blog Tag Typo
Multiple articles tagged "custtom neon" (double T). Should be "custom neon".

### 8. No JSON-LD Schema Markup
- No `LocalBusiness` schema on homepage
- No `ArtGallery` schema 
- No `Product` schema enhancements beyond Shopify defaults
- No `FAQPage` schema on service pages
- No `Event` schema for gallery events
