## 90-Day SEO Plan

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)

#### 1.1 Technical SEO Audit
- [ ] Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- [ ] Verify all product pages are indexed (site:coconutbikini.com)
- [ ] Check for crawl errors in GSC
- [ ] Ensure canonical tags are correct on all pages
- [ ] Add structured data: Organization, Product (with price, availability, reviews), BreadcrumbList
- [ ] Set up Google Search Console and connect to Semrush for tracking

#### 1.2 On-Page Optimization — Existing Pages
- [ ] Optimize homepage title: "Coconut Bikini | Premium Swimwear & Beach Lifestyle Brand"
- [ ] Optimize homepage meta description with call-to-action
- [ ] Add H1 tags to all collection pages if missing
- [ ] Write unique meta descriptions for top 20 product pages
- [ ] Add alt text to ALL product images (keyword-rich, descriptive)
- [ ] Ensure every product has a unique, detailed description (not copy-paste)

### Phase 3: Authority Building (Weeks 7-12)

#### 3.1 Backlink Campaign
Current: 151 backlinks / 90 referring domains. **Target: 140 domains by Week 12.**

**Strategies:**
- **Fashion event PR:** Every Swim Week, casting call, or runway show should generate 3-5 press links. Compile all past coverage and request links from publications that mentioned but didn't link.
- **Lux Neon cross-link:** Coconut Bikini events at Lux Neon Art Gallery = natural reciprocal link opportunity.
- **Ambassador program:** Require ambassadors to link back from their social bios / blogs.
- **Fashion directories:** Submit to swimwear brand directories, indie fashion listicles, and "best of" roundups.
- **HARO / Connectively:** Respond to journalist queries about swimwear, fashion trends, Miami lifestyle.
- **Guest posts:** Write for Miami lifestyle blogs, fashion publications, beach/travel sites.

#### 3.2 Local SEO (Miami/South Florida)
- Claim Google Business Profile if applicable (pop-up shop, Lux Neon partnership)
- Target "bikini shop miami", "swimwear dania beach", "coconut bikini miami"
- Create a "Visit Us" page highlighting the Lux Neon gallery connection

#### 3.3 Social Signals & Brand Searches
- Increase branded search volume by driving social engagement
- Use Instagram Reels, TikTok content from fashion shows
- Cross-promote with Lux Neon events
- Ambassador program = decentralized content creation
