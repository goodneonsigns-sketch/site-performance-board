## The "Dodgers Template" — Scale Strategy

The Dodgers corset is proof of concept. Here's how to replicate it across every team:

1. **Product page:** Fully optimized with unique description, schema, internal links
2. **Collection page:** Team-specific with all related products
3. **Blog post:** "Best [Team] Game Day Outfits" style content
4. **Social post:** Customer wearing the piece at a game
5. **Backlink:** Share in team fan communities

Repeat for: Eagles, Dolphins, Cowboys, Steelers, Packers, Rams, Panthers, Heat, Lions, and expand to new teams as products are created.

**One team fully optimized = 3-5 ranking keywords. 10 teams = 30-50 keywords. That's the path from 33 to 300+ keywords.**
