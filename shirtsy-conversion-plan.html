Retention Strategy
6 Programs to Build a Repeat Customer Engine

From zero to a full lifecycle — starting with free Klaviyo flows this week.

📧
Program 1
Post-Purchase Email Sequence
  • Day 0: Order confirmation + "What to expect" guide
  • Day 3: "Your order shipped!" + sneak peek of new products
  • Day 7: "How did your order turn out?" + review request
  • Day 14: 10% off next order coupon (expires in 7 days)
  • Day 30: "Missing us?" + 15% off or free shipping
  • Channel: Email + SMS
🟢 Week 1 — Free via Klaviyo flows. Zero cost to start.
🎉
Program 2
First-Time Buyer Welcome Offer
  • After first order ships: "Welcome to the Shirtsy family!"
  • 15% off second order (code: WELCOME15)
  • Expires in 30 days — creates urgency
  • Goal: Convert one-timers to two-timers (the hardest step)
  • Projected: Convert 10% of one-timers → +22 repeat customers/month
🟢 Week 1 — Minimal cost, massive impact on repeat rate.
📦
Program 3
BOGO / Bundle Deals
  • Buy 24+ of same item, get 10% off (volume discount)
  • "Team Bundle": 12 tees + 6 hoodies = free shipping
  • "Event Pack": Order 50+ items, get 5 free
  • Seasonal flash sales (Back to School, Holiday, Spring Teams)
  • Goal: Increase AOV from $101 → $150+
🟡 Week 2–3 — WooCommerce coupon setup required.
👑
Program 4
Loyalty / Reorder Rewards
  • Points system: $1 spent = 1 point · 100 pts = $5 off
  • Bonus: 2× points on reorders within 30 days
  • VIP tiers: Bronze (1–2 orders), Silver (3–5), Gold (6+)
  • Gold members: free shipping + priority production
  • Goal: Create habit loop, make switching costly
🔵 Month 2 — Needs WooCommerce loyalty plugin setup.
🔁
Program 5
Win-Back Campaigns
  • 30 days no order: "We miss you" + 10% off
  • 60 days no order: "Your credit line is still active" + 20% off
  • 90 days no order: "Last chance" + 25% off + free item
  • SMS + Email combo for max reach
  • Goal: Recover 15% of dormant customers
🟡 Week 2 — Klaviyo flows + WooCommerce coupons.
🤝
Program 6
Referral Program
  • "Give $10, Get $10" — refer a business, both get $10 credit
  • Referral link embedded in post-purchase emails
  • Leaderboard for top referrers (gamification)
  • Goal: New customer acquisition at $10 CAC (vs paid ads at $30+)
🔵 Week 3–4 — Needs plugin or custom code integration.
