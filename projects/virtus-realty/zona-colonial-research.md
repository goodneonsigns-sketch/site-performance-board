# Zona Colonial Research Notes

## Overview
- **Full name**: Ciudad Colonial (Colonial City) / Zona Colonial / "La Zona"
- **Location**: West bank of the Ozama River, central Santo Domingo, Distrito Nacional
- **Size**: 1.06 km² (0.41 sq mi), walled perimeter
- **UNESCO World Heritage Site** since 1990
- **Oldest continuously inhabited European settlement in the Americas**
- Founded by Bartholomew Columbus (late 15th century), relocated to west bank by Nicolás de Ovando after 1502 hurricane

## Key Historical Landmarks
- **Catedral de Santa María la Menor** — First cathedral in the Americas (16th century)
- **Alcázar de Colón** — Palace of Diego Columbus (son of Christopher), restored 1950s
- **Fortaleza Ozama** — Oldest military fortification in the Americas
- **Calle Las Damas** — Oldest paved street in the New World (1502), cobblestone
- **Parque Colón** — Central square with Columbus statue
- **Puerta del Conde** — Historic gate, where Dominican independence was declared (1844)
- **Monasterio de San Francisco** — Ruins of first monastery in the Americas
- **National Pantheon** — Former Jesuit church
- **Museo de las Casas Reales** — Former governors' palace
- **Casa de Ovando** — Now luxury hotel Hodelpa Nicolas de Ovando

## Real Estate Market (2026)
- **Property types**: Spanish Colonial courtyard homes, restored apartments, gated condo developments, boutique hotels
- **Price range**: $100K-$300K+ USD for apartments/condos, higher for colonial homes
- **Airbnb nightly rate**: $65-$85/night average
- **Gross rental yield (Santo Domingo)**: 7.63%
- **Average Airbnb revenue**: ~$13,215/year, 53% median occupancy
- **Tourism**: DR hit record 11.8M visitors in 2025
- **Property prices rising**: 10%+ in past year
- **Foreigners**: Same property rights as Dominican citizens

## Investment Appeal
- UNESCO protection ensures preservation and prestige
- Tourism foot traffic (main tourist destination in Santo Domingo)
- Growing expat/retiree community
- Ongoing government infrastructure improvements (Integral Revitalization Plan)
- CONFOTUR tax exemptions available
- Proximity to beaches: Boca Chica (25 min), Juan Dolio (40 min), Punta Cana (2 hrs)
- Short-term rental demand strong

## Lifestyle
- Cobblestone streets, outdoor cafes, restaurants, art galleries
- Dynamic nightlife, culturally diverse population
- Museums, theaters, live music
- Walking distance to everything within the zone
- Close to modern Santo Domingo (malls, hospitals, offices)

## Nearby Neighborhoods

- **Piantini** — Upscale commercial district (highest prices in SD)
